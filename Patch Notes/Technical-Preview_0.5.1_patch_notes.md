## 0.5.1 — UX tightening and updater groundwork
**Focus**
- Improve the live user experience and reduce awkward startup/update behavior.

**Features**
- Killmail intel banner introduced/refined to reflect local data freshness.
- Background / passive local intel update direction became part of the app flow.
- Settings tab and dark mode entered the product direction.
- Board workflow became more operationally readable.

**Fixes**
- Startup and update-flow issues reduced.
- Better handling of “one day behind” / “latest published archive” logic.
- Early truthfulness improvements in how freshness/update state was shown.

**Other changes**
- 0.5.1 was a meaningful bridge between the earlier tech-preview shape and the fuller 0.6.x experience.
